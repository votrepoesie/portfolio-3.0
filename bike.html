E-BIKE DASHBOARD

Making sense of semi-autonomous e-bikes for urban commuters.

PROBLEM

Envisioning level-4 autonomy for micro-mobility vehicles to increase user adoption.

As car use decreases among Gen Z, Ford Motor Company wants to move into markets that would better serve younger customers, who are more likely to purchase electric vehicles. My team was tasked with envisioning new controls for semi-autonomous electric bikes.

SOLUTION

Prioritizing safety and ease of use with intuitive controls, in & out of autonomous mode.
