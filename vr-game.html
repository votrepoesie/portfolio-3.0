VR GAME FOR SELF-COMPASSION

Empowering young adults to manage stress and overcome feelings of inadequacy.

LOGISTICS
Design Lead

I led a team of 3 designers and 2 researcher for five months.

I was in-charge of setting meeting agendas, goals and directions for prototyping, and ensuring the final designs were technically feasibility.

PROBLEM

42% of Gen Z has been diagnosed with a mental health condition, with anxiety being the most common diagnosis.

To better understand how young adults feel about processing their emotions and their mental wellbeing, we conducted six focus groups with 2-3 participants each. We used New Metaphors to help participants express how they feel.

Overall, we found that 80% of participants need help with overcoming self-critical thoughts, which hinder confidence, exacerbate anxiety and depression, and impede personal growth.

SOLUTION

Using persuasive design to foster self-compassion and emotional resilience.

REFLECTION

Be scrappy. Take risks. Use whatever means to quickly test the idea and be fearless of technological constraints, because we can learn from failures and compromises.
